### C) Material Interaction Profiles

> Material does not create abilities. It filters tone and mechanical bias.

**Weapon Material Bias**

| Material | Bias | Tone |
|----------|------|------|
| Metal | Control / Structure | push control, anchor, mark, interrupt, anti-air, brace triggers |
| Bone / Hide | Aggression / Instinct | bleed, prone punisher, pursuit triggers, charge bonuses |
| Stone / Crystal | Resonant / Elemental | detonation, elemental rider, terrain burst, hazard zone |

> ⚠ These are tone definitions only. Full mechanical effects go in the tables below.

**Armor Material Bias**

| Material | Bias | Tone |
|----------|------|------|

| Hide / Bone | Mobility / Aggression | movement-trigger riders, recovery speed, anti-prone mobility |

> No flat AR bonuses beyond weight class.

### D) Common & Rare Effect Tables

> All crafted items may contain: 1 Common effect + 1 Rare effect (if rare part used). Never more. No cross-monster stacking. No dual rare stacking.

**Common Weapon Effects** *(GM chooses 1)*
1. Effect 1
2. Effect 2
3. Effect 3 *(optional)*

Rules: repeatable / no new actions / no cooldown mechanics / no stat bonuses / must obey material bias

**Rare Weapon Effects** *(If rare part used — GM chooses 1)*
1. Rare Effect 1
2. Rare Effect 2

Rules: may grant special action (1/combat or stamina-costed) / may grant unique reaction / may modify movement rules / may introduce terrain interaction — no flat stat bonuses, no permanent damage boosts, no attribute changes

---

**Weapon Archetype Translation Rules (Strict)**

**🏹 Bow → New Arrow Type**
- Always becomes a new arrowhead option
- Limited uses per hunt (2 unless otherwise specified)
- Uses Bow DC
- Fully integrated into Arrow Swap system

**🪄 Wand → Affinity Only** *(unless explicitly stated in Rare effect)*
- Gains the monster's Affinity tag
- Affinity defines: Hex type / damage type / visual expression
- No new rider, no new detonation rule, no new bonus effect
- Only if a Rare effect explicitly grants a special action does the Wand convert it into a spell-rider expression

**🔷 Conduit → Affinity = Access to Aspect**
- Gains the monster's Affinity
- Unlocks that Affinity's Aspect (no modified or buffed version)
- Rare terrain-related effect may slightly modify the Aspect expression — not its magnitude

**🎵 Flute → New Ballad**
- Rare effect converts into a new Ballad
- Delivered via normal Ballad rules at the same mechanical strength
- Aura expression instead of single-target

### E) Signature Craft *(Rare Required)*

> One per monster. Identity anchor, not a power escalation.

```
Requires:  Rare part
Type:      Replaces standard armor template OR defines a specific weapon variant
Rules:     Cannot stack additional rare effect / cannot stack another signature
           May modify AR by ±1–2 within its defined template
Identity:  Must represent the monster's strongest expression
```
